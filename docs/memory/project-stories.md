# Project Stories

STAR-format stories from verified experience.
Agents pull these for interview prep, resume bullets, and outreach.

Do not fabricate. Do not inflate. Every story must be defensible in an interview.

---

## Story Format

Each story follows STAR:
- **Situation:** Context. What was the state of things?
- **Task:** What was your specific responsibility?
- **Action:** What did you personally do? (Use "I", not "we")
- **Result:** What measurably changed? (With data if possible)

Tag each story with: skill domains, applicable roles, impact level (Low / Medium / High / Critical).

## Notes for Agents

- Pull the most relevant story for each interview question.
- Do not combine stories into a fictional composite.
- Do not alter the result — if the outcome was ambiguous, say so.
- When a question requires a skill not covered here, flag it: "No verified story for [domain] — flag for human."
